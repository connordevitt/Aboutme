System Administration Best Practices

Tools, Techniques, and Insights

By Connor Devitt

As a system administrator, managing a secure and efficient IT environment is both a challenging and rewarding responsibility. Over the years, I've honed my skills using tools like Google Workspace, Google Admin, and Automox to streamline operations, enforce policies, and maintain robust patch management. In this blog, I’ll be sharing my insights and best practices to help fellow administrators optimize their workflows.

One of the core areas I plan to cover is user management within Google Workspace. From setting up new accounts to managing access permissions, the goal is to ensure that users have the resources they need while maintaining a secure environment. I’ll also delve into the importance of enforcing consistent policies through Google Admin, which can greatly enhance security and ensure compliance across the organization.

Patch management is another critical aspect of system administration, and I’ll be sharing my experiences with Automox in this area. Properly managing patches is vital to protecting against vulnerabilities, and Automox has been a powerful tool in automating this process. I’ll discuss strategies for effective patch management, including scheduling, testing, and reporting.

In addition to tools and techniques, I’ll also share some personal experiences and lessons learned from my time in system administration. Whether you’re managing a small business or a large enterprise, I believe there’s always something new to learn and share.

Stay tuned as I explore these topics in greater detail, providing actionable insights and tips that you can apply in your own system administration practices.
